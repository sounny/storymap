Step 3

Create a Public Account

Follow these steps to complete your registration:

  1. Click "Create a public account" (not the organizational option)
  2. Choose your sign-up method:
    • Sign up with Google
    • Sign up with Apple
    • Sign up with Facebook
    • Or use your email address
  3. Fill in your details:
    • First and last name
    • Email address
    • Username (choose something professional!)
    • Password (8+ characters, mix of letters and numbers)
  4. Accept the Terms of Use and click "Create Account"

Step 4

Verify Your Email

Check your email inbox for a verification message from ESRI:

  1. Open the verification email (check spam/junk if needed)
  2. Click the verification link in the email
  3. You're done! Your account is now active

Step 5

Access StoryMaps

Now that your account is set up, let's access the StoryMaps platform:

  1. Go directly to StoryMaps: storymaps.arcgis.com ‚Üí
  2. Click "Sign In" in the top right corner
  3. Enter your credentials that you just created
  4. Welcome to your StoryMaps dashboard!
