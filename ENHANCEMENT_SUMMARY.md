## Pedagogical Framework

### Geographic Learning Progression

**Module 1:** Foundation - Access geospatial tools and community

**Module 2:** Platform Mastery - Understand StoryMaps as geographic communication tool

**Module 3:** Applied Learning - Create geographic narrative about meaningful place

**Module 4:** Capstone Project - Demonstrate geographic thinking through spatial storytelling

---

## Core Geographic Competencies Emphasized

1. **Spatial Thinking** - Reading maps, identifying patterns, understanding location
2. **Place-Based Analysis** - Understanding unique characteristics and meaning of locations
3. **Spatial Communication** - Conveying geographic information effectively
4. **Data Interpretation** - Making sense of geographic evidence
5. **Scale Awareness** - Understanding local to global connections
6. **Ethical Practice** - Responsible representation of places and communities

The workshop now explicitly connects StoryMaps to:

## Assessment & Outcomes

Students completing this workshop will be able to:

✓ Think geographically about places, phenomena, and spatial relationships
✓ Create interactive, narrative-driven geographic maps
✓ Combine qualitative and quantitative geographic evidence
✓ Communicate complex spatial concepts to diverse audiences
✓ Build portfolio-quality geographic projects
✓ Apply geospatial tools to real-world geographic challenges
✓ Engage ethically with places and communities through storytelling
